AI and Data Bootcamp

Data is everywhere and data is the fuel for artificial intelligence. In this four hour intensive hands-on bootcamp we shall explore the following:

Hour 1 and 2: What is data? How to explore the data through basic statistics and visualization?

Hour 3 and 4: What is artificial intelligence? How to use the data to do supervised and unsupervised machine learning?

By the end of this boot camp you will have basic foundational knowledge of what is artificial intelligence and how can you use it to predict with the historical data.
